Iran hits US oil tanker in the Gulf: State TV
Iranian state television said Iran had struck a US oil tanker in the Gulf with a missile on Thursday, the latest Iranian attack on the energy industry in the region.
For all the latest headlines follow our Google News channel online or via the app.
The ship “was hit by a missile in the north” of the Arabian Gulf and “is currently on fire,” Iran’s Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps said in a statement reported by state television.
The incident, which has not been independently confirmed, came as the IRGC said they had “full control” of the Strait of Hormuz, which connects the Gulf to the Indian Ocean and is a vital oil and gas transit route.
