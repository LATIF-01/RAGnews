Saudi Arabia intercepts, destroys three cruise missiles, several drones
Saudi Arabia’s defense ministry announced on Thursday that the Kingdom intercepted and destroyed a number of cruise missiles and drones amid ongoing regional tension. 
For all the latest headlines, follow our Google News channel online or via the app.
Three cruise missiles were intercepted and destroyed outside the city of al-Kharj, the ministry said citing its spokesperson. It added that Kingdom also intercepted and destroyed three drones east of al-Kharj Governorate. 
The spokesperson also noted that a drone was intercepted and destroyed east of al-Jouf Region in the north of the Kingdom.
